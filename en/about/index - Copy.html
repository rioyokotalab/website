About

Events in the group

We have weekly meetings on topics ranging from parallel programming paradigms, performance analysis tools, parallel debugging, continuous integration tools, to advanced topics in algorithms, linear algebra, and numerical analysis. You will be gradually introduced to the various research topics, along with the basic research skills like how to read and write papers, and how to plan your research. Opportunities for scholarships, contests, internships, and overseas studies will be provided.

How to join the group

If you are applying from outside Japan, there are a few different programs for overseas students. Please see the following page.
