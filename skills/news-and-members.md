# Skill: News and member pages

News (top page + news/):
- Only top-conference acceptances and grants. No workshops, talks, or
  low-tier venues unless explicitly approved.
- Add BOTH the dated top-page row and the anchored news entry, in BOTH
  languages. Use the announcement date and the member's grade at that date.

Members:
- Removing a member also PREPENDS them to Alumni on both member pages;
  JP format: `姓 名 (Romaji Name)`.
- Never rewrite historical news or publications for status changes.
- Member CV sections (受賞歴/委員歴/研究課題 on jp/member/yokota.html) are the
  canonical exporter source; the EN page mirrors them but is not parsed
  (see skills/exporters.md).
